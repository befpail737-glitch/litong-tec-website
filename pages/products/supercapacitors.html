CRRC Supercapacitors

High-power density supercapacitors for energy storage, regenerative braking, and peak power applications. Millions of charge/discharge cycles with minimal degradation.

Next-Generation Energy Storage

CRRC supercapacitors (also known as ultracapacitors or EDLCs) bridge the gap between traditional capacitors and batteries, offering unique advantages for specific applications.

With power densities up to 100 times higher than batteries and the ability to charge/discharge in seconds, supercapacitors are ideal for applications requiring rapid energy transfer and long cycle life.

Key Advantages:

  • Millions of charge/discharge cycles
  • Charge in seconds, not hours
  • Wide operating temperature range
  • Maintenance-free operation
  • Environmentally friendly (no heavy metals)
  • High reliability and safety

Key Applications

🚇 Regenerative Braking

Capture and store braking energy in rail vehicles, buses, and trucks. Reuse for acceleration, reducing energy consumption by up to 30%.

⚡ Grid Stabilization

Fast frequency response and voltage regulation for power grids. Smooth renewable energy fluctuations.

🏭 Peak Power Support

Provide burst power for industrial equipment, reducing peak demand charges and avoiding grid upgrades.

🔋 Hybrid Energy Storage

Combine with batteries to extend life and improve performance in EVs and stationary storage systems.

🚗 Cold Start Assist

Reliable engine starting in extreme temperatures for trucks, buses, and heavy equipment.

📡 Backup Power

Short-term backup for critical systems, providing seamless transition to generators during outages.
