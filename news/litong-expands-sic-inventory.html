LiTong Expands SiC Module Inventory to Meet Surging EV Market Demand

By LiTong Supply Chain Team | Published on: August 15, 2025 | Category: Company News

In response to unprecedented growth in the 800V electric vehicle sector, LiTong has significantly increased its stock of CRRC's automotive-grade SiC modules, ensuring faster delivery times for our key customers. This strategic inventory expansion reflects our commitment to supporting the EV industry's rapid transition to wide-bandgap semiconductors.

Market Demand Driving Inventory Expansion

Our supply chain team has observed remarkable growth in demand for SiC solutions:

  • 800V Platform Adoption: Major automakers are rapidly adopting 800V architectures for faster charging and improved efficiency.
  • Increased Order Volumes: We've seen a 300% increase in SiC module orders year-over-year.
  • Lead Time Pressure: Factory lead times have extended due to high demand, making strategic inventory critical.

New Inventory Commitments

Our expanded inventory includes key CRRC Times Electric SiC products:

T-SiC-M1200 Automotive SiC MOSFET

This automotive-grade SiC MOSFET is designed for main inverters in electric vehicles:

  • 1200V blocking voltage with low on-resistance
  • AEC-Q101 qualified for automotive applications
  • Optimized for high-frequency switching in 800V systems
  • Enhanced thermal performance for reliable operation

New Stock Level: Increased from 5,000 units to 25,000 units across our global distribution centers.

T-SiCSBD-A1200 SiC Schottky Diode

This companion diode is optimized for use with SiC MOSFETs in high-efficiency applications:

  • 1200V rating with excellent reverse recovery characteristics
  • Low forward voltage drop for minimal conduction losses
  • High-temperature operation capability
  • Matched thermal characteristics with T-SiC-M1200

New Stock Level: Increased from 8,000 units to 40,000 units.

Global Distribution Strategy

Our expanded inventory is strategically positioned across key markets:

  • Asia-Pacific Hub: Shenzhen facility holds 50% of total inventory for rapid delivery to Chinese and Southeast Asian customers.
  • European Hub: Munich facility holds 30% of inventory to serve European automotive manufacturers.
  • Americas Hub: Detroit facility holds 20% of inventory for North American EV producers.

Benefits for Our Customers

This inventory expansion delivers tangible benefits to our customers:

  • Faster Delivery: Standard delivery times reduced from 8-12 weeks to 2-4 weeks for stocked items.
  • Supply Chain Stability: Reduced risk of project delays due to component shortages.
  • Cost Optimization: Volume pricing available for customers utilizing our expanded inventory.
  • Technical Support: Our FAE team provides design assistance for SiC implementation.

Future Investment Plans

We're continuing to invest in our SiC capabilities:

  • Additional Products: Planning to stock CRRC's upcoming 6500V SiC modules for charging infrastructure.
  • Regional Expansion: Evaluating new distribution centers in key EV markets.
  • Technical Resources: Developing application-specific design guides for SiC implementations.

Conclusion

LiTong's significant investment in SiC module inventory demonstrates our commitment to supporting the electric vehicle industry's transition to wide-bandgap semiconductors. By ensuring availability of CRRC Times Electric's advanced SiC solutions, we're helping our customers accelerate their EV development programs while reducing supply chain risks.
