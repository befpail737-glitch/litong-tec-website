Rail Traction Inverter Solution

By LiTong Solutions Engineering Team | Solution Type: Transportation | Application: Rail Transit

Our complete rail traction inverter solution leverages CRRC Times Electric's field-proven T-IGBT-H3300 modules to deliver exceptional performance, reliability, and efficiency for high-speed trains, metros, and electric locomotives. This solution has been deployed in over 10 million kilometers of railway operation worldwide.

Solution Overview

The rail traction inverter solution is designed to convert DC catenary power to variable frequency AC for precise motor control in electric railway systems. Key features include:

  • High Reliability: Built with T-IGBT-H3300 modules rated for >1,000,000 power cycles
  • Efficiency Optimization: Low conduction and switching losses for reduced energy consumption
  • Regenerative Braking: Seamless energy recovery during braking operations
  • Wide Operating Range: Operates reliably from -40°C to +70°C ambient temperatures

Core Components

The solution is built around CRRC Times Electric's advanced power semiconductor portfolio:

T-IGBT-H3300 Rail Transit Module

The heart of the solution, the T-IGBT-H3300 delivers uncompromising performance in demanding railway environments:

  • 3300V blocking voltage with 1200A continuous current capability
  • Low saturation voltage (2.4V typ.) for minimized conduction losses
  • Press-pack configuration for reliable pressure contact assembly
  • Railway qualified to IEC 61373 vibration and shock standards

Complementary Components

  • Gate Drive Units: Isolated gate drivers optimized for T-IGBT-H3300 switching characteristics
  • DC-link Capacitors: Low-inductance capacitors for stable DC bus voltage
  • Current Sensors: Hall-effect sensors for precise motor current measurement
  • Control Board: DSP-based controller with real-time motor control algorithms

System Architecture

The traction inverter system architecture is optimized for railway applications:

  • Modular Design: Easily configurable for different power requirements
  • Redundancy: Parallel module configuration for improved reliability
  • Cooling System: Double-sided cooling with liquid cold plates
  • Protection Features: Comprehensive fault detection and protection algorithms

Performance Benefits

Customers implementing our rail traction inverter solution report significant improvements:

  • Energy Efficiency: 3-5% improvement in overall system efficiency
  • Reliability: 50% reduction in inverter-related maintenance events
  • Performance: Enhanced dynamic response for improved passenger comfort
  • Lifecycle Cost: Extended service intervals reduce total cost of ownership

Application Examples

Our solution has been successfully deployed in diverse railway applications:

  • Urban Metro: City subway systems with frequent start-stop cycles
  • Freight Locomotives: Heavy-haul operations requiring high torque
  • Light Rail: Low-floor trams and intercity rail services
