Product Description

The T-IGBT-W1700 is designed to meet the rigorous demands of multi-megawatt wind turbine and solar farm inverters. It features low VCE(sat) for high-efficiency power conversion and is packaged in an industry-standard housing for easy integration. The module is optimized for power cycling capability, ensuring a long operational lifetime even under the fluctuating power generation conditions of renewable sources.

Key Features:

  • High Power Cycling Capability
  • Low Conduction and Switching Losses
  • Industry Standard Housing
  • High-Reliability Al2O3 Substrate
  • Enhanced Field-Stop Trench Gate Technology
