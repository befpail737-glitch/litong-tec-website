Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to your questions. If you can't find what you're looking for, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Are all your products original and authentic?

Yes. As an officially authorized core distributor for CRRC Times Electric, we guarantee that 100% of our products are original, brand-new, and fully traceable back to the factory. We can provide certificates of conformity and other documentation upon request. All our products come with our authenticity guarantee.

How can I request a sample?

For qualified projects, we are happy to provide engineering samples. The best way is to navigate to the specific product page you are interested in and click the "Request a Sample" button. Alternatively, you can use our main Contact Us page and specify the part number and project details. Our team will review your request and respond within 24 hours.

Do you provide technical support?

Absolutely. Technical support is a core part of our value proposition. Our team of experienced Field Application Engineers (FAEs) can help with component selection, design review, troubleshooting, and understanding datasheets. Please contact us with your technical query. We offer support in multiple areas including:

  • Product selection and application guidance
  • Design review and optimization
  • Simulation and modeling assistance
  • Failure analysis and troubleshooting

What is your typical lead time?

For items we have in stock, we can typically ship within 1-2 business days. For factory orders, the lead time varies depending on the specific product and factory capacity. When you request a quote, we will provide you with an accurate and up-to-date lead time. Our inventory team regularly updates stock information to ensure accuracy.

Do you offer volume pricing or discounts?

Yes, we offer competitive volume pricing for qualified customers. The specific discount structure depends on the product, quantity, and delivery schedule. Please contact our sales team with your volume requirements, and we will provide a customized quote. We also offer special pricing for long-term framework agreements.

Can you help with custom packaging or labeling?

Yes, we offer value-added services including custom packaging, labeling, and kitting. This is particularly useful for OEM customers who need specific labeling or packaging configurations. Please discuss your requirements with our customer service team when placing your order.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including bank transfer, credit card, and letter of credit for qualified customers. Payment terms depend on your customer status and order value. New customers typically start with prepayment or letter of credit, while established customers may qualify for net payment terms.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship worldwide. We have extensive experience with international shipping and can handle all necessary export documentation. Shipping times vary by destination, but we typically use express courier services for urgent deliveries. For large shipments, we can arrange air or sea freight as needed.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns for unused, unopened products within 30 days of delivery. The product must be in its original packaging and in resalable condition. A restocking fee may apply. Please contact our customer service team to initiate a return. We cannot accept returns for custom or special order items.
