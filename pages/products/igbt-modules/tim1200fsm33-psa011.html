What is the CRRC TIM1200FSM33-PSA011 IGBT Module?

The TIM1200FSM33-PSA011 is a high-power IGBT (Insulated Gate Bipolar Transistor) module manufactured by CRRC Times Electric Co., Ltd., a leading Chinese power semiconductor company and subsidiary of CRRC Corporation—the world's largest railway rolling stock manufacturer.

This module belongs to CRRC's TIM series of high-voltage IGBT modules, specifically engineered for demanding industrial and transportation applications requiring reliable power conversion at high voltages and currents.

Key Identifiers

  • Manufacturer: CRRC Times Electric Co., Ltd.
  • Part Number: TIM1200FSM33-PSA011
  • Series: TIM (High-Voltage IGBT Modules)
  • Package: Standard 34mm half-bridge module
  • Technology: Trench-gate field-stop IGBT with soft-recovery diode
