Sensor Product Range

Current Sensors

Hall effect current sensors for non-contact current measurement.

  • Range: ±50A to ±2000A
  • Accuracy: ±0.5% to ±1%
  • Isolation: Up to 5kV
  • Bandwidth: Up to 100kHz
  • Output: Analog, Digital, PWM

Applications: EV motor control, battery management, solar inverters

Voltage Sensors

High-voltage isolation amplifiers for voltage monitoring.

  • Range: Up to 1500V DC
  • Accuracy: ±0.5%
  • Isolation: Reinforced isolation
  • Response: < 10μs
  • Package: Compact SMD

Applications: Battery voltage monitoring, DC bus sensing

Temperature Sensors

NTC and PT temperature sensors for thermal management.

  • Range: -40°C to +150°C
  • Accuracy: ±1°C to ±3°C
  • Type: NTC thermistors, PT100/1000
  • Package: Various mounting options
  • Response: Fast thermal response

Applications: Motor temperature, battery thermal management
