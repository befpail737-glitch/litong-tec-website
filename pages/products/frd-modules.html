CRRC FRD Modules

Fast Recovery Diode modules optimized for high-frequency rectification and inverter applications. Superior reverse recovery characteristics with voltage ratings up to 6500V.

High-Frequency Rectification Solutions

CRRC Fast Recovery Diodes (FRDs) are designed for applications requiring fast switching speeds and low reverse recovery losses. Our FRD modules combine multiple diode chips in robust packages for high-power applications.

With reverse recovery times as low as 50ns and soft recovery characteristics, these diodes minimize switching losses and EMI in high-frequency power converters.

Key Benefits:

  • Ultra-fast reverse recovery (trr < 200ns)
  • Soft recovery characteristics reduce EMI
  • High surge current capability
  • Low forward voltage drop
  • Robust module packages
  • Wide voltage range: 600V to 6500V

FRD Technology Features

⚡ Fast Recovery

Reverse recovery times from 50ns to 200ns enable high-frequency operation up to 100kHz, reducing passive component size.

🌡️ High Temperature

Operating junction temperatures up to 175°C with low leakage current, ensuring reliability in demanding applications.

💪 Surge Capability

High non-repetitive surge current ratings (IFSM up to 10kA) provide excellent protection against transient overloads.

📦 Module Packages

Industry-standard packages (34mm, 62mm, hockey puck) ensure compatibility with existing designs and heatsinks.

🔧 Parallel Ready

Positive temperature coefficient and matched characteristics enable easy parallel connection for higher currents.

✓ AEC-Q Qualified

Automotive-grade options available for EV onboard chargers, DC-DC converters, and traction inverters.
