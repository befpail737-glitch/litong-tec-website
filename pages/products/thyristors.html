CRRC Thyristors & IGCTs

High-power thyristors and Integrated Gate-Commutated Thyristors (IGCTs) for phase control, HVDC transmission, and industrial power applications. Voltage ratings up to 8500V.

Power Control Solutions

CRRC thyristors and IGCTs represent the pinnacle of high-power semiconductor technology, enabling efficient control of megawatt-level power in demanding applications.

Our phase control thyristors excel in AC power control applications, while IGCTs combine the high-power handling of thyristors with the convenience of gate turn-off capability.

Key Benefits:

  • Ultra-high voltage capability (up to 8500V)
  • Massive current handling (up to 6000A)
  • Low on-state losses
  • High surge current capability
  • Proven reliability in HVDC applications
  • Gate turn-off capability (IGCT)

Product Range

Phase Control Thyristors

Standard thyristors for AC power control and rectification.

  • Voltage: 1200V - 8500V
  • Current: 100A - 6000A
  • Package: Hockey puck (Press pack)
  • Features: Low on-state voltage
  • Applications: Soft starters, rectifiers

Inverter Grade Thyristors

Fast-switching thyristors for forced commutation applications.

  • Voltage: 1200V - 4500V
  • Current: 200A - 4000A
  • Package: Hockey puck
  • Features: Fast turn-off time
  • Applications: Inverters, choppers

IGCTs

Integrated Gate-Commutated Thyristors with turn-off capability.

  • Voltage: 4500V - 6000V
  • Current: 1000A - 4000A
  • Package: Press pack with gate unit
  • Features: Gate turn-off, low loss
  • Applications: MV drives, traction
