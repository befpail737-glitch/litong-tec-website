About LiTong Electronics

Your trusted partner for CRRC Times Electric power semiconductors, delivering excellence in power electronics distribution across China and beyond.

Our Mission

LiTong Electronics is a leading, officially authorized core distributor for CRRC Times Electric (tec.crrczic) Power Semiconductors. We are committed to providing our customers with the highest quality power semiconductor solutions for industrial, automotive, and renewable energy applications.

Why Choose LiTong?

  • Authorized Distributor: Official partnership with CRRC Times Electric ensures authentic products and full manufacturer support
  • Technical Expertise: Our experienced engineers provide comprehensive technical support and application guidance
  • Quality Assurance: ISO 9001 certified processes guarantee product authenticity and reliability
  • Comprehensive Inventory: Extensive stock of IGBT modules, SiC devices, FRDs, and power management solutions
  • Fast Delivery: Strategic locations across China enable rapid response and delivery
